Shower moisture and waterproof wall and flooring

Single person shower installation and waterproofing

Shower built with TiLite Showerpan Underlayment and TiLite XPS Foam Waterproof Backer Board Underlayment. These panels are lightweight and easy to install by one person. Ready to install tile, no preperation needed. I ordered these online at Home DePot and picked them up at the local store. The best shower panels I have ever used ...lightweight and waterproof.

Installation of the rock tile: The tile is held with Sika Construction Adhesive. This was much more quicker and simpler to level. For the smaller trim rocks I used the Sashco Clear Lexel ( waterproof ).
